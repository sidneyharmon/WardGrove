About Us

Ward Grove Farm has been in our family since the 1830's growing cotton, soybeans, corn, and livestock. In 1986 the first Christmas trees were planted. In 1990, choose and cut sales began with a farm wagon at the center of operations. With the addition of our rustic cabin and mule-driven wagon rides in 1993, families began looking to our farm as an important Christmas tradition.

We are open from 9 until dark on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday and noon until dark on Sunday. We will be open by appointment on Monday through Wednesday. We will cut your tree, shake the needles, and bale it for easy handling. All children receive a candy cane as well as a coloring book.

Our Products

We grow two species of pine trees, Virginia and White Pine. All trees 10 feet and over are $7.50 a foot.

The needles of the Virginia Pine are soft and range from 1.5 to 3 inches in length. This tree has a pleasant pine fragrance and good needle retention. Virginia Pine trees are $6.00 per foot.

The needles of the Eastern White Pine tree are soft and flexible and 2.5 to 5 inches in length. Needle retention is excellent. The White Pine has a mild pleasant fragrance. White Pine trees are $6.50 per foot.
